Frequently asked questions
Can I hire someone to put furniture together?
Absolutely! Shubham can connect you with handy Workers who are experienced in all kinds of furniture assembly.
How much does it cost to assemble furniture?
Workers offer furniture assembly services at rates from ₹400-500 per hour. If you need any old furniture removed, ask your Worker if they can bring their vehicle to help you!
How long does it take to assemble furniture?
Furniture assembly typically takes between 1-4 hours depending on the size and complexity of the furniture.
Is flat pack furniture easy to assemble?
Flat pack furniture is delivered ready to assemble with all the instructions and pieces needed to build it. Assembling it can be difficult for many people, so it's okay to hire help.
Do I need to provide tools to Taskers for furniture assembly?
Generally, workers provide their own equipment, but it helps to confirm with them prior to the job whether they have the right tools. If your furniture assembly job requires power tools, be sure to let your Worker know beforehand if possible.
